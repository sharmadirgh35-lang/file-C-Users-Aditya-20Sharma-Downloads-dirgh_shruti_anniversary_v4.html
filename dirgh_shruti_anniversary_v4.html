My dearest Shruti,

Two years ago, on a day I never want to forget, you walked into my life and quietly made it whole. I didn't know then that a single moment could hold the weight of everything — but with you, every ordinary day became something I wanted to remember forever.

Two years, and I would choose you again in every single one.

Forever yours, Dirgh 🌹
